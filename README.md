# law-and-ai-reading-list
A curated and annotated collection of resources on law, artificial intelligence, human rights, and digital governance.

## About this project

This repository brings together legal instruments, policy documents, academic research, reports, and educational resources for exploring the relationship between law and artificial intelligence.

Rather than collecting links without context, each resource will include a short description, its main topic, jurisdiction, level of difficulty, and an explanation of why it matters.

## Main topics

- AI regulation and governance
- Human rights and emerging technologies
- Algorithmic bias and discrimination
- Privacy and data protection
- Transparency and explainability
- AI in courts and public institutions
- AI and education
- Access to information
